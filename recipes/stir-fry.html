Stir fry

An easy stir fry dish Click here for the original recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 sliced red bell pepper
  • 1 sliced yellow bell pepper
  • 1 cup of sugar snap peas
  • 1 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup baby corn
  • 2 cups of broccoli
  • 1/2 cup of water chestnuts
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 3 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • Optional garnish: chopped green onions and sesame seed

Cooking instructions:

  1. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to the wok over medium to high heat. Add peas, bell pepper and all other vegetables listed in the above ingredients list.
  2. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are near tender.
  3. Add the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic chicken broth, sesame oil and cornstarch to a small bowl and whisk.
  4. Pour the above mixture over the vegetables and cook in the wok until the sauce has thickened. Optionally, garnish with the chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
